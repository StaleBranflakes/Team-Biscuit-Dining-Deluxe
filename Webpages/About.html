Our Vision

Dining Deluxe is a student-driven way to experience campus dining at UNCP. Built with inclusivity and innovation in mind, our platform helps you make confident food choices that match your lifestyle, preferences, and dietary needs.

Whether you're navigating allergies, exploring plant-based options, or just curious about what dishes are available, Dining Deluxe gives you the tools to stay informed.

  • 🍽️ Discover daily menus across all campus dining locations
  • 🌿 Filter meals by allergens, dietary preferences, and restrictions—vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.
  • 🤖 Get personalized meal suggestions powered by AI, based on your input (diet, allergies, cravings, etc.)
  • 🔍 Search for specific ingredients or dishes to find what fits your lifestyle
  • 🚫 Stay updated on ingredient availability—staff and students can flag shortages in real time

Dining Deluxe also offers tailored access:

  • Students: View menus, apply filters, and receive personalized suggestions
  • Staff & Dining Teams: Update inventory, mark items as low or out of stock, and keep the system responsive

We believe campus dining should be clear, customizable, and collaborative.
